Complete analytics for every manufacturing function

Executive manufacturing overview

Get a single-screen view of your entire operation. Track OEE, OTIF, scrap rate, scrap cost, and inventory value alongside monthly production trends with built-in forecasting. Designed to give plant heads and leadership real-time answers without drilling through multiple reports.

Inventory, supply chain & cost analytics

Monitor supplier lead times, inventory turns, and days on hand alongside CPU, material cost, and labor cost trends. Track GRN acceptance vs rejection by month, supplier-wise rejection quantities, and item-wise cost contribution to spot margin leakage early.

Production performance

Drill into the three OEE pillars, availability, performance, and quality, alongside machine utilization and throughput hours. Compare actual vs ideal cycle time, monitor plan vs actual production by month, and break down output by morning, evening, and night shifts to identify where output is being lost.

Quality analytics

Track first pass yield, defect rate, in-process defect rate, and rejection quantity at the machine and item level. Break down defects by category (assembly, dimensional, surface, process, packing), monitor region-wise customer complaints, and use clustering to map rejection rate against rework cost.

Maintenance & machine health

Monitor breakdown counts, MTTR, breakdown probability, and high-risk machine count in one view. Compare preventive vs breakdown maintenance over time, track spare parts consumption cost, and rank machines by total maintenance cost and average downtime to prioritize where to act first.
