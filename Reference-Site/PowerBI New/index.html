Frequently asked questions

How hard is it to switch from Power BI to Zoho Analytics?

  • Not hard at all. Most teams are up and running in days. Zoho Analytics supports importing your Power BI datasets and rebuilding dashboards with our easy drag-and-drop interface.

Will I lose my data or dashboards?

  • No. Your data remains secure and accessible. While dashboards can't be auto-converted, you can easily recreate reports with similar or improved functionality (often with fewer clicks).

Why is Zoho Analytics the best Power BI alternative in 2025?

  • With Zoho Analytic's user-friendly interface, integration with more than 500+ connectors, and pocket friendly pricing, it stands out as the best Power BI alternative in 2024. The advanced analytical capabilities, flexible deployment options, and advanced security make it a top choice for businesses looking for an easy, scalable, and customizable BI solution.

Can Zoho Analytics connect to the same data sources?

  • Yes, and then some more. Zoho Analytics supports 500+ data connectors, including databases, data lakes, data warehouses, business apps, and more.

What about user roles and permissions?

  • You're covered. Zoho Analytics offers granular user-level controls, data sharing permissions, and role-based access.

Is Zoho Analytics secure and enterprise-ready?

  • Absolutely. We're GDPR compliant, SOC 2 Type II certified, and support SSO, encryption, and IP restrictions. Big or small, your data stays protected.

What kind of support do I get during migration?

  • You will get onboarding help, live chat, documentation, and even dashboard rebuilding support with the professional services program. We're here to make migration painless.

Is there a free alternative to Power BI?

  • Zoho Analytics has a free plan that includes basic data analysis and reporting features, making it an excellent alternative to Power BI for users seeking an analytical tool with no cost. This option is perfect for individuals and small teams interested in exploring BI capabilities without an initial financial commitment.

How does Zoho Analytics compare to Power BI in terms of pricing?

  • Compared to Power BI, Zoho Analytics offers a more budget-friendly pricing plan. The recent increase in Power BI's pricing - from $10 to $14 for Power BI Pro and from $20 to $24 for Power BI Premium Per User - has prompted many users to seek a more affordable yet equally powerful analytical tool.
