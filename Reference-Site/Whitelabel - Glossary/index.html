What is White Label BI

White-label business intelligence (BI), refers to the provisioning of analytics platform capabilities that are seamlessly integrated within another host application. This approach ensures that businesses can present real-time insights under their own branding.

Importance of White-Labeling in BI & Analytics

Brand Consistency

Blending analytics seamlessly with the host application's UI-achieving uniformity in design elements such as fonts, colors, and layout builds familiarity and trust among existing users.

User Adoption

Delivering a smooth, integrated experience where users access analytics within familiar interfaces fosters greater engagement and improves user adoption

Competitive Advantage

White-labeling analytics allows businesses to offer advanced, proprietary capabilities that enhance differentiation and strengthen competitive positioning.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between white-label and embedded analytics?

  • Embedded analytics refers to integrating dashboards or reports directly into your existing application or website. In contrast, white-label analytics allows you to fully rebrand the analytics experience of an existing platform, making it appear as your own solution.

Can I white-label analytics on my own domain?

  • Yes. White-label BI platforms—especially Zoho Analytics—facilitate hosting the analytics interface on your own custom domain (e.g., analytics.zylker.com). This enables a seamless, fully branded experience from start to finish, without any third-party URLs or vendor references.

What customization options are available?

  • White-label BI platforms like Zoho Analytics offer extensive customization options, including:
    • Visual Branding
    • Custom Domains
    • Tailored dashboard & report layouts
    • Role-based access control
    • Extensive localization
    • Integration with a wide variety of sources
    • Support for APIs, SDKs, and iFrame embed options
  • This flexibility ensures that the analytics experience aligns perfectly with your brand specific needs

Is white-label BI suitable for small businesses?

  • Absolutely. White-label BI and analytics is ideal for small and medium-sized businesses that want to offer advanced analytics without the cost or complexity of in-house development. It enables them to deliver branded, professional-grade insights at scale to clients, partners, or internal teams.
