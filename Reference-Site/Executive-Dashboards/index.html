How to create an executive dashboard in Zoho Analytics

Ready-to-use executive dashboard templates and examples for every function

Here are the four executive dashboards our customers build most often. Each comes with a pre-built template, connects to your existing tools, and refreshes automatically.

Executive Sales Dashboard

A sales executive dashboard in Zoho Analytics pulls directly from your CRM and gives you a live view of pipeline health, stage-by-stage conversion, rep performance, and forecast vs. actuals, without rebuilding a report every time you need to check.

KPIs typically tracked: Pipeline value, win rate, average deal size, quota attainment by rep, revenue forecast vs. target, deals by stage, average sales cycle length etc.

View sales dashboard templates →

Executive Finance Dashboard

Finance dashboards in Zoho Analytics connect to your accounting software and pull revenue, expenses, cash flow, and P&L in real time. You can also blend your financial data with CRM data to see which customer segments drive the most profitable revenue, not just the highest top-line number.

KPIs typically tracked: Revenue vs. budget, gross margin, cash flow, accounts receivable aging, operating expenses by category, profit and loss by product line, budget variance etc.

View finance dashboard templates →

Executive Marketing Dashboard

Marketing data is scattered across more tools than almost any other function. Organic traffic in Search Console. Paid spend in Google Ads and Meta. Email in HubSpot or Mailchimp. Pipeline attribution in the CRM.

A marketing executive dashboard blends all of these into one view so you can see the full picture from first touch to closed deal, and actually answer where your best leads come from.

KPIs typically tracked: CAC by channel, marketing-sourced pipeline, MQL to SQL conversion, ad spend vs. revenue, email performance, website traffic by source, SEO trend etc.

View marketing dashboard templates →

Executive Helpdesk and Operations Dashboard

When support data lives alongside CRM data, you can answer harder questions than "how many tickets did we close?" Which customer segments generate the most support volume? Which product lines have the highest defect rates? Where are you spending support resources on accounts already at churn risk?

KPIs typically tracked: First response time, resolution rate, SLA compliance, CSAT score, support volume by category, cost per ticket, ticket trend by product line, etc.

Finance dashboard FAQs

What is an executive dashboard?

  • An executive dashboard is a single-screen view of your business's most important KPIs, pulled from multiple data sources and updated automatically. It gives leadership a quick read on performance across sales, finance, marketing, and operations without digging through individual reports.

What should be in an executive dashboard?

  • The essentials are revenue vs. target, pipeline health, gross margin, CAC, and operational KPIs relevant to your business. The goal is breadth over depth, enough to spot what's off plan and know where to investigate further, all from one view.

How do I build an executive scorecard or report?

  • In Zoho Analytics, connect your data sources, pick a pre-built template, and it populates with your numbers instantly. You can also use the drag-and-drop builder or tell Ask Zia what you need, and it generates the full dashboard from a single prompt.

What are the leading executive dashboard software in 2026?

  • Zoho Analytics, Tableau, and Power BI are among the most widely used. Zoho Analytics stands out for combining depth, AI capabilities, and 500+ native connectors at a price point that works for both mid-market and enterprise teams.

How do executive dashboard software tools work?

  • They connect to your data sources, sync data on a schedule you set, and display it as live charts, KPI widgets, and tables. Executives can filter, drill down, and share views without touching the underlying data or waiting on a report to be built.

Can executive dashboard tools handle real-time data?

  • Yes. Zoho Analytics supports real-time sync for many connectors, with hourly and daily options for others. For most executive use cases, a daily sync is sufficient. For live operations or revenue-critical tracking, real-time is available and straightforward to configure.

What to look for in a CEO dashboard software for executives?

  • Prioritize breadth of data connectors, ease of use without technical help, cross-source data blending, mobile access, and flexible sharing controls. AI-powered querying is increasingly important, as it lets executives ask questions directly rather than waiting for someone to build a new report.
