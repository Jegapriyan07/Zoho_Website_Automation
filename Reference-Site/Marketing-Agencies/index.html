Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to get started?

  • Most marketing agencies are fully set up in under a week. Connecting your tools and customizing your white-labeled portal is fast and requires little to no developer involvement.

Can I white-label everything?

  • Yes. Your portal will reflect your brand entirely, including logo, color scheme, domain, and email sender. There’s no visible mention of Zoho anywhere, unless you want it.

Can I manage reporting for multiple clients?

  • Absolutely. You can create separate workspaces for each client, manage access rights, and reuse dashboard templates.

Do I need a developer to embed or customize the portal?

  • No developers needed. Everything can be configured through a visual interface. Embedding dashboards or setting up a branded portal is simple and guided.

What about data security and privacy?

  • Zoho Analytics is fully compliant with GDPR and other major data protection frameworks. You have full control over who can see what, down to individual reports and data fields.

Can clients log in to view dashboards on their own?

  • Yes. You can give clients secure, role-based access to your white-labeled portal, so they always have the latest insights without having to ask you for a report.

Can I schedule recurring reports?

  • Yes. You can automate delivery of dashboards, charts, and even custom-written insights via email or Slack.

What kind of insights does the AI offer?

  • Zoho’s AI agent, Ask Zia, can answer natural language questions (like “Which campaign had the best ROI?”), highlight anomalies, and forecast future trends, helping you and your clients stay ahead.
