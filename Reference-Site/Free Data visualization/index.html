Frequently asked questions

Is Zoho Analytics' free plan really free? Are there any hidden costs?

  • Yes, our free plan is completely free to use with no hidden costs or credit card required. You get access to essential features including 10,000 rows of data, unlimited reports and dashboards, AI-powered analytics, and collaboration for up to two users.

What types of data sources can I connect to?

  • You can import data from over 100 sources, including:
    • Spreadsheet files (Excel, CSV)
    • Cloud storage (Google Drive, Zoho Workdrive)
    • Databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL)
    • Data warehouses and data lakes
    • Real-time data feeds

Do I need technical skills to use this software?

  • No technical expertise is required. Our intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to create visualizations and reports. The AI-powered features help automate complex analyses and make data visualization accessible to everyone.

Can I upgrade to a paid plan later?

  • Yes, you can easily upgrade to a paid plan anytime to access additional features like Zia, our AI assistant, more user licenses, increased rows, business app integration with pre-built reports and dashboards, and advanced collaboration tools.

What kind of support is available for free users?

  • Free plan users have access to:
    • Comprehensive documentation and tutorials
    • Community forums
    • Email support
    • Video guides

Can I access my dashboards on mobile devices?

  • Yes, you can view and manage your reports and dashboards on-the-go using our dedicated mobile apps for both Android and iOS devices. The mobile apps are free to download and use.

How often is the data updated?

  • With the free plan, you can schedule automatic data updates daily. The system will refresh your data sources according to your specified schedule, and your reports and dashboards always reflect the latest information.
