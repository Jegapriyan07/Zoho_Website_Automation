FAQs
What is a sales dashboard?
A sales dashboard is a visual report that pulls your CRM and sales data into one screen. It tracks metrics like revenue, pipeline, deal stages, and rep performance in real time, so you don't have to dig through spreadsheets to know where things stand.
What should a sales dashboard include?
At minimum: total revenue, open pipeline, deals won and lost, conversion rate, and average deal size. Many sales teams also add rep performance, lead source breakdown, and sales cycle length depending on what they're managing.
Can I build a sales dashboard without knowing SQL or coding?
Yes. Zoho Analytics lets you connect your CRM and build dashboards using drag-and-drop and Ask Zia, the AI agent. You don't need to write any queries or formulas to get started.
Can I share my sales dashboard with people outside my team?
Yes. You can share dashboards with specific users via email, embed them in internal portals, or generate a public link with view-only access. You control permissions for each share.
Can multiple salespeople use the same dashboard with filtered views?
Yes. You can set up user filters so each rep only sees their own deals and metrics when they log in, while managers see the full team view on the same dashboard.
