FAQs

What is client dashboard software?

  • Client dashboard software lets agencies pull marketing data from every channel into one live, branded dashboard each client can access. Instead of building manual reports, you give clients a real-time view of their campaign performance they can check anytime.

Can I white-label dashboards with my agency's branding?

  • Yes. Zoho Analytics lets you apply your agency's logo, colors, and domain to every client dashboard, so clients open a report that looks like your own product rather than third-party software you resell.

How many data sources can I connect?

  • Zoho Analytics connects 500+ data sources, including 30+ marketing tools like Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta, and LinkedIn, plus databases, data warehouses, and spreadsheets. You can blend all of them into a single cross-channel view.

Can clients access their own dashboards?

  • Yes. Each client gets secure, role-based access to a live dashboard showing only their own data. They can filter, drill into details, and answer their own questions without emailing you or scheduling a call.

Does it automate client reporting?

  • Yes. Zoho Analytics syncs your data on a schedule and emails each client a branded report on their own cadence, so the monthly report goes out automatically without your team rebuilding it by hand each time.

How does the AI help with client reporting?

  • Zia, the built-in AI, answers plain-English questions, generates summaries on top of each report and dashboard, and flags why a metric changed. Clients get the context behind the numbers.

Is Zoho Analytics good for agencies managing many clients?

  • Yes. You can run separate business dashboards for each client, control access individually, and reuse templates to set up new accounts quickly, so reporting scales as your client roster grows.
