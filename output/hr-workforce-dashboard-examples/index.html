HR Overview Dashboard

Track your complete workforce at a glance. This dashboard gives HR managers a quick read on headcount, new hires, attrition, and quarter-over-quarter trends in one view.

Key KPIs

  • Total headcount
  • New hires
  • Attrition rate
  • Average tenure

Employee Attendance Dashboard

Monitor attendance and leave patterns across the organization. Spot absenteeism trends, track average leave taken, and compare on-time vs late check-ins by month.

Key KPIs

  • Average attendance rate
  • Total leaves taken
  • Absenteeism rate
  • Average late check-ins
  • Leave balance by department

Recruitment Dashboard

See how your hiring pipeline moves from application to offer. Identify which sources deliver the best candidates and flag roles with open requisitions sitting idle.

Key KPIs

  • Open requisitions
  • Time to hire (days)
  • Offer acceptance rate
  • Candidates per source
  • Interview-to-offer ratio

Employee Performance Dashboard

Understand how performance ratings, goals, and reviews trend across teams. Track review completion and identify top performers as well as employees who need support.

Key KPIs

  • Average performance rating
  • Review completion rate
  • Goals achieved
  • Top performers count
  • Performance by department
  • Rating distribution

Attrition & Retention Dashboard

Compare attrition across departments, tenure bands, and managers. Quickly identify flight-risk segments and measure the impact of retention programs.

Key KPIs

  • Voluntary vs involuntary attrition
  • Attrition by department
  • Regretted attrition
  • Average tenure at exit
  • Retention rate
  • Exit reasons breakdown

Diversity & Inclusion Dashboard

Get a clear picture of workforce diversity across gender, age, and tenure. Track how representation changes over time and where gaps remain at each level.

Key KPIs

  • Gender diversity ratio
  • Diversity by level
  • Diversity in new hires
  • Pay equity ratio
  • Representation trend

Payroll & Compensation Dashboard

Analyze payroll cost, compensation bands, and benefits spend across the organization. Useful for budget planning and compensation review cycles.

Key KPIs

  • Total payroll cost
  • Average compensation by role
  • Compensation band adherence
  • Overtime cost
  • Benefits spend by category

Workforce Planning Dashboard

Get a historical and current view of workforce capacity. Track headcount forecasts, span of control, and role vacancies broken down by department and quarter.

Key KPIs

  • Forecasted headcount (this quarter)
  • Vacancy rate
  • Span of control
  • Internal mobility rate
  • Headcount by department
  • Hiring plan vs actuals

FAQs

What is an HR dashboard?

  • An HR dashboard is a visual report that pulls your HR system and workforce data into one screen. It tracks metrics like headcount, attrition, recruitment, and performance in real time, so you don't have to dig through spreadsheets to know where things stand.

What should an HR dashboard include?

  • At minimum: total headcount, attrition rate, open positions, average tenure, and time to hire. Many people teams also add performance ratings, diversity metrics, and payroll cost depending on what they're managing.

Can I build an HR dashboard without knowing SQL or coding?

  • Yes. Zoho Analytics lets you connect your HR system and build dashboards using drag-and-drop and Ask Zia, the AI agent. You don't need to write any queries or formulas to get started.

Can I share my HR dashboard with people outside my team?

  • Yes. You can share dashboards with specific users via email, embed them in internal portals, or generate a public link with view-only access. You control permissions for each share.

Can multiple HR team members use the same dashboard with filtered views?

  • Yes. You can set up user filters so each manager only sees their own department's data when they log in, while leadership sees the full organization view on the same dashboard.
