How is Zoho Analytics different from the built-in reports in Zoho Books?

  • Zoho Books includes standard financial reports like P&L, balance sheet, and tax summaries, which are great for day-to-day accounting. Zoho Analytics goes further: it lets you build custom dashboards, blend your Books data with CRM or Expense data, track cash flow trends over time, and share live reports with your team or leadership. You get a much deeper view of what's actually driving your financial performance.

Can I integrate Zoho Analytics directly with my Zoho account?

  • Yes. Zoho Analytics has a native integration with Zoho Books. Once connected, it automatically syncs your invoices, expenses, payments, and other financial data and generates 150+ pre-built reports and dashboards to get you started instantly. Setup takes just a few clicks.

Do I need technical knowledge or coding skills to use Zoho Analytics?

  • Not at all. Zoho Analytics offers a drag-and-drop interface for building reports and dashboards. No SQL or coding required for most use cases. You can also use Ask Zia, the AI agent, to generate reports by simply typing a question like "What were my top expenses last quarter?"

What types of insights can I get that I can't see in Zoho Books?

  • With Zoho Analytics, you can track overdue invoice trends by customer segment, compare revenue vs. expenses month-over-month, forecast cash flow, identify your most profitable customers or regions, monitor payment collection cycles, and blend your accounting data with sales or marketing data for a complete business picture.
