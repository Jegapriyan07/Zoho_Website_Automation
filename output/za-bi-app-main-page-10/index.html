Experience the truly native mobile BI apps of Zoho Analytics

Crafted for Android and iOS, the Zoho Analytics Mobile Apps delivers a seamless and responsive experience that takes full advantage of each platform's unique features.

Here's what makes the Zoho Analytics Mobile apps stand out:

  • Customized dashboard layout

    Reorder, resize, and custom design your own dashboards to fit in your business needs. Quickly access and visualize the most relevant data and monitor key metrics with flexible layout options.

  • Zia Insights

    With Zoho's intelligent assistant, Zia, gain deeper insights through data analysis and natural language querying. Perform predictive analysis and much more with the natively built Ask Zia to make informative decisions.

  • Smart filters and drill-downs

    Explore your data in greater detail. Use interactive gestures like tap, swipe, pan, etc., to discover hidden patterns and trends to drive better business outcomes.

  • Favorites and recent views

    Bookmark your most frequently used reports and dashboards and simplify your workflow. The favourites feature, combined with the recent views section, comes in handy to navigate to the information you need.

  • Enhanced security

    By leveraging the security features of iOS and Android, Zoho Analytics Mobile Apps employs advanced encryption techniques to protect your data both in transit and at rest.

  • Performance and compatibility

    The Zoho Analytics Mobile Apps are tailor-made to work perfectly with the hardware and software configurations of your device. This results in a more reliable and stable application that is less prone to crashes and other issues.
