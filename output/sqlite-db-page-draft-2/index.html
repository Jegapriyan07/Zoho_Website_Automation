What is SQLite?

SQLite is a lightweight, self-contained, and serverless database engine. It is widely used for embedded applications, mobile apps, and small to medium-scale data storage needs. SQLite does not require a separate server process unlike traditional database management systems, making it easy to deploy and maintain. When integrated with Zoho Analytics, deeper insights can be unlocked with your SQLite data by transforming it into meaningful reports and dashboards.

How does Zoho Analytics and SQLite bring value together?

Zoho Analytics is a self-service, AI-powered BI platform that enables you to seamlessly integrate your SQLite data, clean, and prepare it for analysis, and gain valuable insights through interactive visualizations. By integrating the two, you can transform raw SQLite data into meaningful reports through interactive visualizations. Together, SQLite and Zoho Analytics empower businesses to efficiently analyze and visualize their data and make informed decisions.

Data Import

Bring in data into Zoho Analytics effortlessly

Bring billions of rows of data seamlessly from your SQLite database into Zoho Analytics in just a few simple steps. You can also schedule your import on an hourly, daily, or weekly basis for automatic data import into Zoho Analytics.

Zoho Analytics offers flexible data importing options. By using Zoho Databridge, you can import data into Zoho Analytics directly by keeping the data in the desired system.

Data Preparation

Enrich the quality of your business decisions

With Zoho Analytics' in-built AI-assisted data preparation tool, you can cleanse, model, transform, enrich, and blend data from multiple sources before analysis. This makes it easy to get your data ready for a smooth analysis.

Live Connect

Set up a live connection with your database

Zoho Analytics offers a Live Connect feature that allows you to create live data visualizations. This means that your data will not be stored in Zoho Analytics servers, and reports will always be up-to-date with the latest data from your SQLite database.
