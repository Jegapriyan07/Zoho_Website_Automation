Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zoho Analytics?

  • Zoho Analytics is a self-service BI and analytics platform that lets you connect your data, build reports and dashboards, and uncover insights without writing code.

Do I need coding or SQL knowledge to use it?

  • No. Most report and dashboard building is drag-and-drop, and you can also ask questions in plain language using the built-in AI assistant.

Can Zoho Analytics connect to my existing tools?

  • Yes. It integrates with 100+ popular business apps and a wide range of databases, files, and cloud data sources.

Can I embed Zoho Analytics inside my own product?

  • Yes. You can white-label dashboards and reports and embed them into your own application using single sign-on or SAML.

Is my data secure on Zoho Analytics?

  • Yes. Data in transit is encrypted with TLS, personally identifying information can be selectively encrypted at rest, and all activity is captured in audit logs.
